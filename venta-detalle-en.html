Built in 2007, “Huerta de las Monjas” is probably the most beautiful villa in Marbella, due to its Andalusian Renaissance stately mansion design, which combines the style of Italian Renaissance villas with the warmth of the Andalusian Renaissance palaces. Doors, beams, fountains, furniture and other items have been acquired over many years in antique shops and demolition of former private palaces. The villa, its gardens, the best panoramic views over the Mediterranean, Africa, Gibraltar, the nature reserve of Sierra Blanca and Marbella, its proximity to the town of Marbella, the motorway access, and the fact of being surrounded by the nature reserve of Sierra Blanca, which is accessed directly from the door of the plot, make it exclusive and unique.
