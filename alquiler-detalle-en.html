AMENITIES AND SPECIAL FEATURES OF THIS VILLA

Fireplace, Terrace, Alarm system, Mountain view, Jacuzzi, Swimming pool, Air conditioning, Basement, Sauna Gym, Tennis court, Guest toilet, Garden, Sea/lake view, Built-in kitchen

The property comprises a main house with seven bedrooms on the upper part and a guest house on the lower plot with another two bedrooms en-suite with a total of approx. 1.100 m² built area. The exclusive and very private property enjoys the most amazing sea views. Everything has been incorporated into the property one can dream of in a Sierra Blanca villa, features like salon with chimney, extra large kitchen with Gaggenau appliances, under-floor heating throughout, cinema room, all done to high specs and in a Moroccan style. There is one guest suite on the main level, three on the first floor with amazing views and three on the lower level. The garage allows for three cars. From the main terrace the manicured gardens and the outdoor heated swimming pool can be accessed with a new decked lounge area. On the lower part of the property are several entertainment areas with a bar, separate guest suite, billiard room and a grand stand overlooking the new hard court tennis court with flush lights for tennis games at night. Next to the tennis court lies the Moorish style guest house with a fully fitted gym and Spa with sauna and Hammam. The focus in the guest house is the honeymoon suite in Moroccan design with all modern features. Through a separate driveway there is the possibility to park 4 cars in front of the guest house.
